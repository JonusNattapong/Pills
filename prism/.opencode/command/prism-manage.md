# prism-manage

Manage custom Prism pills. Create, edit, delete, disable, enable, list.

## Usage

`/prism-manage` — then describe what you want to do

## Examples

- "list my custom pills"
- "create a pill called ninja for stealth coding"
- "edit the cobalt pill, change its emoji"
- "disable the storm pill"
- "delete the test-pill"
